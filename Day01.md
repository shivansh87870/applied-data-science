## What is Data Science?

Data Science is the process of converting raw data into meaningful insights that help in making better decisions.

It combines:

* Statistics
* Programming
* Machine Learning
* Data Visualization
* Domain Knowledge

In simple terms, data is everywhere — Data Science helps us make sense of it.

---

## Data Science Lifecycle

Raw Data → Collection → Cleaning → EDA → Modeling → Insights → Decision

---

## Real-World Applications

### Early Disease Detection

Data Science helps in analyzing patient history and medical scans to detect diseases at an early stage.
This allows doctors to start treatment sooner and improve survival rates.

**Real-world impact**

* Early diagnosis
* Better treatment planning
* Reduced healthcare costs

---

### Fraud Detection

Banks and financial companies use Data Science models to identify unusual transactions.
If something suspicious happens, the system flags it immediately.

**Real-world impact**

* Prevents financial fraud
* Protects customers
* Improves security systems

---

### Recommendation Systems

Platforms like Netflix, Amazon, and Spotify recommend content based on user behavior.
These systems analyze what users watch, search, or purchase.

**Real-world impact**

* Personalized user experience
* Increased engagement
* Better product discovery
